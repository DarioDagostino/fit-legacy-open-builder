# Monetization Notes

This project is best positioned as a low-friction sharing tool for coaches and fitness creators.

## Likely Customer

The strongest commercial segment is not the casual end user. It is the coach or small fitness business that already sends routines through WhatsApp, PDFs, screenshots, or spreadsheets.

## Core Free Value

- Create a routine.
- Generate a share link.
- Recipient opens without installing an app.

This free flow should stay simple.

## Potential Paid Features

- Branded routine links.
- Saved client library.
- Routine templates.
- Analytics for opened/completed routines.
- Custom domains or branded landing pages.
- Team or coach workspace.
- Export history.

## Avoid

- Monetizing basic link opening.
- Requiring account creation for recipients.
- Positioning the product as a generic fitness app.
- Over-selling AI as the main value.

## Pricing Hypothesis

Start with a coach-facing plan. Validate willingness to pay only after the free sharing workflow is reliable and easy to explain.
